Homemade Pizza

ingredients:

  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 200gr shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pizza toppings (olives, mushrooms, etc.)

The process:

  1. The dough: Mix flour, sugar, yeast, warm water, and olive oil in a bowl until a dough forms. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Roll & Shape: Roll the dough out onto a baking sheet into a round or rectangular shape.
  3. Add toppings: Spread the tomato sauce evenly, sprinkle the mozzarella cheese, and add your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake: Bake in a preheated oven at 220°C for about 12-15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
